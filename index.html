About InAmigos Foundation

Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit organization committed to creating positive social change across India. Since 2020, we've been working tirelessly to make a difference in the lives of underprivileged communities.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through education, skill development, and sustainable initiatives that create lasting impact.
